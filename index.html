The problem

Every autonomous agent today borrows credentials from its operator — a shared credit card, no visibility, no per-agent limits. When the agent spends $4,000 on API calls at 3am, no one knows until the bill arrives.

Agents need their own financial identity. Not a delegation from a human's card. Their own account, their own card, their own spend history. Owned by the operator. Controlled by policy. Auditable by design.
